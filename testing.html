    Description

    Essentially a box that bullets come out of, the MAC-10 SMG boasts a high rate of fire, with poor spread accuracy and high recoil as trade-offs. It has been custom painted with the image of a deadly biker. One part firearm, two parts Trapper Keeper

    History

    The MAC-10 | Neon Rider was first introduced to CS2 10 years ago, on April 15th, 2015. It was released as part of the Chroma 2 Case, alongside the "Chromatic Scale" update. The skin was created by the community designer "Puffin".

    Origin

    The MAC-10 | Neon Rider can be obtained by opening a Chroma 2 Case container. The skin is also part of the The Chroma 2 Collection.

    Popularity

    With a popularity of 99%, the MAC-10 | Neon Rider is currently extremely popular, which makes it one of the most popular items in CS2. This popularity measurement is based on the daily sales volume and the price of the skin.

    Rarity

    The MAC-10 | Neon Rider is one of 283 other SMG skins. The rarity of the skin is Covert, which makes the Neon Rider a super rare drop with an estimated drop chance of only 0.64%.

    Availability

    With a price range between $95.98 and $111.42, the MAC-10 | Neon Rider is a rather affordable skin. Fortunately, it's currently widely available and can be purchased on multiple different markets.

    Versions

    The float value of the MAC-10 | Neon Rider ranges from 0.00 to 0.45, which makes the skin available in Factory New, Minimal Wear, Field-Tested and Well-Worn. For each exterior, there also exists a StatTrak version for the Neon Rider.

    Finish Style

    This MAC-10 has been painted in a "Custom Paint Job" style, with the Neon Rider finish. This style enables extremely customized looks in a full range of colors. The skin has been painted in a color scheme of cyan and pink. Different pattern indexes have no effect on the Neon Rider finish. The skin uses the old legacy model.

    Community Sentiment

    The MAC-10 | Neon Rider has received over 8.1K votes on CSGOSKINS.GG, leading to an average rating of 4.7 out of 5 stars. The skin is currently also owned by the professional CS2 players coldzera, happy and shox.
